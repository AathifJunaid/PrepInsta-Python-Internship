Robotic Arm

The robotic arm is powered by a NodeMCU, which acts as its brain. Using the Arduino software, we program it to perform various tasks. The coolest part? We control its movements effortlessly through the Blynk app on our phone. With its five degrees of freedom, it can precisely maneuver to pick up and place objects wherever we need them.
